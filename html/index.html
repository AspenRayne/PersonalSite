About

So who am I as a Full-Stack Developer?

Well, I graduated from an intensive 60-70 hour week programming bootcamp. It was the best decision I have ever made. I learned how OOP (Object Oriented Programming) works with the language I know the most; Java. I implemented Java in Spring Framework and Spring Boot. I also learned and worked with relational databases and used the following querying languages: SQL, JPA, JDBC, and JPQL. I built and interacted with REST APIs using JSON. On the client side, I learned and used Angular, JavaScript, TypeScript, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.

In three of my projects, I incorperated third party API's such as: Google Find Places API, Seatgeek API, and the Kroger API. I am most proud of these full-stack applications in particular because it taught me a lot about dependency injection and importing Java libraries. JSON-simple was especially useful to keep my code readable and efficient when managing deeply nested JSON payloads in Java.

Now who am I as a person?

Since I was little girl, I always wanted to do something with technology, it always fascinated me. So here I am, making my dreams come true. As a new and inspired Full-Stack Developer I know there is a lot more for me to learn and that is what I am most excited about. I love taking on challenges and I am a very determined and curious person. I am currently expanding my knowledge and building awesome applications.
